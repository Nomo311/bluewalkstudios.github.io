Greetings. My name is Ryan Cooper. I'm a Game Designer & Developer who specializes in Mechanical Storytelling and Narrative Worldbuilding.

Though I wear many crowns, my talents and passions lie in creating powerful stories with video games as the conduit. When I was young - born and raised in Gary, Indiana - the breathtaking worlds I found myself in are what gave me solace, and the experiences I took from them are what I aim to reflect in my creative expression.

I obtained my Associate's Degree in IT from Vincennes University, and I am expecting my degree in Media Arts & Science from Indiana University Indianapolis (IUI) come Spring 2025.
